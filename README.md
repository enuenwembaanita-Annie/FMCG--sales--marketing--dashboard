# FMCG Sales & Marketing Profitability Dashboard

## Project Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analysing sales,marketing and profitability performance for an FMCG business across products,geography, pricing and sales performance covering 2023 to 2025.

## Dataset
- fmcg_sales_marketing_profitability_2023_2025.xlsx
- Period: 2023 - 2025

## Dashboard Pages
- Page 1 — Product Performance Analysis
- Page 2 — Geographical Analysis
- Page 3 — Pricing & Marketing
- Page 4 — Sales Performance
- Page 5 — Insights & Recommendations

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: $14.45M
- Total Profit: $3.31M
- Total Units Sold: 4M
- Profit Margin: 23%
- Marketing Spend: $1.56M

## Key Findings
- Instant Coffee leads all products in revenue
- Beverages is the top category at $3.7M
- Europe is the highest revenue region at $4.81M
- USA is the top country at $1.73M
- December is consistently the strongest month
- No Promotion strategy generates the highest 
  revenue at $5.63M
- Oliver Kent leads the team in revenue and profit
- Oceania sales reps record the lowest profit 
  margin at 19.16%

## Recommendations
- Increase Beverage and Instant Coffee inventory 
  ahead of Q4 to capitalize on December peak
- Replicate HomeNest's approach across 
  underperforming brands
- Prioritize investment in European markets
- Investigate low 19.16% profit margin in Oceania
- Reduce spend on low-efficiency promotions like 
  Introductory Offer
- Share Oliver Kent's sales strategy across 
  the wider team as a performance benchmark
